Graduate Account

Graduate Account at a glance

  • Help finding your feet with a tiered interest and fee free Planned Overdraft of up to £2,000 in your first year after graduation, £1,500 in the second year and £1,000 in the third year. All overdrafts are subject to application and approval.
  • A low-cost graduate loan of up to £10,000 to get you started.

  • A Classic credit card with a limit of at least £500
  • 0% commission on foreign currency and non-sterling travellers cheques

How to apply

To apply for a Graduate Account, just visit a Lloyds Bank branch and we’ll open an account for you.

To be eligible you must:

  • be a permanent UK resident and have confirmation of graduation
  • have graduated within the last three years with a degree from a UK-based university
  • transfer your current account from your existing bank if you're not already a Lloyds Bank customer.
You'll need to bring some things with you:
  • a 10-year passport or driving licence and proof of graduation
  • a statement from your current bank or copy of a utility bill dated within the last three months
  • bank statements from the past six months and evidence of your overdraft limit
  • your current debit card and letter of employment confirming your income (if this isn't included on the statements).

Switching made easy

Whether you want to move to Lloyds Bank or you already bank with us and want to open a new account, switching is easy. Our dedicated welcome team will do everything they can to help your switch run smoothly, including transferring all your Direct Debits and standing orders to your new account for you. We will:

  • contact your other bank for a list of your Direct Debits and standing orders
  • tell you when your transfer is due to complete
  • set up your standing orders and ask your service providers to start claiming your Direct Debits from your Lloyds Bank account after your transfer date
  • keep you informed of how your switch is progressing and let you know what you need to do and when.
